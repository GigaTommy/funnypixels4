FunnyPixels is a global co-creation pixel map where your GPS movements become pixel art. Walk, run, or bike through cities and watch your path transform into vibrant pixels on a shared world map.

KEY FEATURES:
- GPS Drawing: Move around to automatically draw pixels on the world map
- Alliance Wars: Join an alliance and conquer territories together
- Drift Bottles: Send messages in bottles that travel between players
- Shop & Customize: Bombs, boost cards, custom flags, and more
- Achievements: Unlock milestones as you explore and create
- Daily Check-in: Earn rewards for daily activity
- Leaderboards: Compete for the most pixels globally and locally

Join millions of players worldwide and leave your mark on the map!
