# FunnyPixels Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 12, 2026
**Version:** 2.0

---

## Introduction

FunnyPixels ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information when you use our application. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Username, email address, and phone number provided during registration
- **Profile Information:** Avatar, display name, bio, and other information you choose to provide
- **Password:** Stored using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt); we never have access to your plaintext password

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Location Data:** Precise GPS location (collected only with your explicit consent for core drawing features)
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (for security and analytics)
- **Usage Logs:** App usage times, feature usage frequency, error logs
- **Pixel Drawing Data:** Coordinates, colors, and timestamps of pixels you draw

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Contacts, SMS, or call history
- Photo library (unless you voluntarily upload a profile picture)
- Continuous background location tracking (location is collected only during active GPS drawing sessions)
- Biometric data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
| Providing core services (pixel drawing, map display) | Performance of contract |
| Account registration and authentication | Performance of contract |
| Security protection and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest |
| Service improvement and product analytics | Legitimate interest |
| Sending important service notifications | Performance of contract |
| Compliance with legal requirements | Legal obligation |

We **do NOT** use your personal information for:
- Automated decision-making or user profiling
- Selling your personal information to third parties
- Marketing communications without your consent

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 **Principle:** We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3.2 **Exceptions:** We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **With your explicit consent**
- **Legal Requirements:** In response to lawful requests from law enforcement, court orders, or legal proceedings
- **Rights Protection:** When necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of FunnyPixels, our users, or the public
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third parties who provide technical support (e.g., cloud service providers), bound by strict data protection agreements
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as a business asset; we will notify you before any such transfer

3.3 **Public Content:** Pixels you draw on the map are visible to all users as a core feature of the service.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- Your data is primarily stored on servers located in the People's Republic of China.
- For users outside mainland China, we will take necessary compliance measures where applicable data protection laws require data localization or restrict cross-border transfers.

### 4.2 Data Retention Periods
- **Account Information:** Retained during your account's lifetime and for 30 days after deletion
- **Pixel Drawing Data:** Retained as part of the public map (public pixel creations)
- **Usage Logs:** Retained for no longer than 12 months
- **Security Logs:** Retained for no longer than 6 months

### 4.3 Security Measures
- Transport encryption (TLS/HTTPS)
- Storage encryption (AES-256)
- Password hashing (bcrypt)
- Access control and permission management
- Regular security audits and vulnerability scanning

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 All Users Have the Following Rights:
- **Right of Access:** View the personal information we hold about you
- **Right of Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information
- **Right of Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent previously given for data processing
- **Right to Account Deletion:** Delete your account
- **Right to Complain:** File a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

### 5.2 Additional Rights for EU/EEA Users (GDPR)
If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you also have:
- **Right to Data Portability:** Obtain a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Right to Restriction of Processing:** Restrict our processing of your data in certain circumstances
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests

### 5.3 Additional Rights for California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)
If you are a California resident, you also have:
- **Right to Know:** Know the categories and purposes of personal information we collect
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)

### 5.4 Exercising Your Rights
To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us:
- Email: privacy@funnypixels.com
- In-App: Settings → Privacy → Data Request

We will respond to your request within **30 days**.

## 6. Location Information

6.1 **Location Data Collection:** We collect precise location information only with your explicit consent and only when you actively use drawing features.

6.2 **Background Location:** GPS drawing mode may require background location permission. This feature activates only when you actively start GPS drawing and you can stop it at any time.

6.3 **Permission Management:** You can modify or revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings. Disabling location permissions may affect drawing features but will not affect map browsing or other features.

6.4 **Location Data Processing:** Location data is used to determine pixel placement coordinates. After processing, only grid coordinates are retained (not precise GPS coordinates).

7.2 We **do not** use third-party advertising tracking cookies.

7.3 You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.
